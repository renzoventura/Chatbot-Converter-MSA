    FAQs

  • How can I sign up for Contoso Bank?

    Moving your banking to Contoso is easy and you can start online. There are so many great reasons to join! Join now by signing up online or booking an appoint in store by dialing 02X-CNTSBNK.

  • How to manage my accounts?

    Sign in with your Contoso Bank Login (if you don't have one sign up now), and go to "Account" page where you can view all your accounts ranging from savings to graduate cheques.

    • Notify Contoso Bank
